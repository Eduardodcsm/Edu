Lore & Timeline

From the first outbreak to the fall of cities, *The Walking Dead* universe spans a wide narrative timeline across TV, games, and comics. This page highlights major events and milestones.

  • Day 0: Outbreak begins. Hospitals flooded. Panic spreads.
  • Day 30: Rick Grimes wakes from his coma.
  • 6 Months Later: Cities fall. Survivors form factions.
  • 1 Year: The world is overrun. Hope becomes survival.
  • Post-Year 2: Conflicts evolve. New enemies arise: The Governor, Negan, and the Whisperers.
  • 10 Years+: Different communities. Crossovers from games like *Telltale's Walking Dead* and *Saints & Sinners* enrich the lore.
