Factions & Safe Zones

In this shattered world, trust is rare. These are the groups that have risen — some to protect, others to control.

  • 🏛️ Alexandria Safe-Zone

    A suburban sanctuary led with democracy and hope. But even fences can’t keep out the darkness forever.

  • 🛠️ Hilltop Colony

    Set on a farm, Hilltop thrives through agriculture and negotiation. Strength through unity.

  • ⚔️ The Saviors

    Survival through fear. Led by Negan, this group imposes brutal rule in exchange for protection.

  • 🌲 The Whisperers

    Walkers are their camouflage. Bound by primal law, this group rejects civilization entirely.

  • 🧬 CRM (Civic Republic Military)

    A secretive, heavily armed organization. Technology. Power. Agenda unknown.
