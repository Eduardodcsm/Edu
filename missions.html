Survival Missions

Every day is a test. Choose a mission. Make it out alive. Or become one of them.

  • 🧟‍♂️ Rescue the Safehouse

    Your group’s supply stash is under siege by roamers. Defend the house or retreat strategically. Supplies: Food, Ammo.

  • 🏃‍♀️ Escape Atlanta

    Navigate through the streets crawling with walkers. Avoid the herd, reach the van, and warn the others.

  • 🔦 Search the Hospital

    Explore the abandoned hospital for antibiotics. But you’re not alone. Silence is survival.

  • 🧭 Track a Traitor

    A member of your group is suspected of betrayal. Track their trail, gather evidence. Justice or mercy?
